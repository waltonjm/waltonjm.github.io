Code Quality and Continuous Improvement

March 23, 2022 | Jacqueline Walton

Programming is a use or lose skill set. It is essential for any person dedicated enough to survive coding boot camp continue to improve their skills sets to ensure code quality.

  1. Maintain a Healthy and Safe Work Environment: Make sure you have an ergonomically correct work station. Proper lighting and keep your music down so you do not destroy your hearing. Lastly, if you have silicone sensitivity or allergy, make sure your headset are not coated in silicone or your in ear headphones contain silicone. You may get ear infections, suffer from inflammation, skin rash etc.
  2. Coding Typing Test: Take 15 minutes every morning and take a coding typing test.
  3. Focus: Seriously, put your headset on with your vibe music and get coding.
  4. Message: Someone always knows more than you. Slack, Discord...whatever method. Just network and contribute to forums such as Stack Overflow.
  5. Read: Programming books, websites, community sites. Then read some more.
  6. Research: Question anything that does not make sense. Research MDN, Stack Overflow, Google for answers. Ask your friends or coworkers for help.
  7. Take Breaks. Take a shower, put on some clean clothes and go have fun. Because once you start --you cannot stop!
