Biography

Jacqueline Walton

Jacqueline Walton is the Founder/CEO of wigwagapp, Inc. The company is set to launch an iOS mobile application, wigwag® a social lifestyle food avoidance management application designed to assist users to live better. Wigwag is Ms. Walton's second startup. She successfully exited her first company in 2016, a government contracting firm she founded in 2005. She took the company from one employee to 450 employees with an estimated 30M in revenue. As the President/CEO, she was responsible for overseeing the day-to-day management including contract and subcontract management while fostering a culture of integrity throughout the company.
